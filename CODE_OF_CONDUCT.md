# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

ULC is developed in the open. Everyone who takes part in this project, in any form, is expected to help keep the collaboration constructive, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in all communication, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Assume good faith when interpreting others' contributions.
- Focus feedback on ideas, designs, and technical tradeoffs, not on individuals.
- Welcome newcomers and help them understand the project.
- Accept constructive criticism and offer it with care.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or intimidation.
- Discriminatory or demeaning language of any kind.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, reviews, or community spaces.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including the repository, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any communication conducted in the context of ULC.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the project maintainers listed in `GOVERNANCE.md`. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially. Maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate, including warnings, temporary limits on participation, or removal from the project's spaces.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant, adapted to the scope of this specification project.
